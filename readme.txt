These folders contain the raw data from the techno-economic analysis in the paper: "The cost of direct air capture and storage: the impact of technological learning, regional diversity, and policy." - Young et al. 2023

The format of the filenames in the folders "cost_breakdown", "learning_curves" and "policy_results" is:

{Technology name}_{Country}_{Electricity source}_{Heat source}_{Policy}.csv
